These patterns have shown to be effective in controlling cart:

Simple Alternation (This maintains state) => 1,2,1,2
Tip Left => 1,1,2,1,1,2
Tip Right => 2,1,1,2,2,1
Hard Tip Left => 1,1,1,2,1,1,1,2
Hard Lip Right => 2,2,2,1,2,2,2,1
Move Left (When pole is tilted right this will move the cart left) => 1,1,1,2,2,1,1,1,2,2
Move Left (When pole is tilted left this will move the cart right) => 2,2,2,1,1,2,2,2,1,1
